# West Bengal Computing Club
Welcome visitor! As the name suggests, we are a computing club based in West Bengal, mostly around Kolkata. We love to create and tinker with technology as well as interacting with the ones who do the same. If you live in WB and think you belong to this group, chances are, we WANT you to be a part of us! We do weekly, monthly and yearly meetups with a dose of chill pill. So lets join this initiative and push general tech-savviness forward in West Bengal!
